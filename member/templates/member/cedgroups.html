What are C.E.D groups?

Christian Education Department (C.E.D) is a department designed for educating peers on the key things that affect them and how to manage them. Peers inconsiderate of the gender meet up and discuss their issues and help each other out. Each group has a designated leader and pastor who leads and oversees the whole meetup.
